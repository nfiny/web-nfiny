Cubo DAO's contract adjusts it's own supply daily to keep a constant APR runway of 365 days. This means that for every node that exists we'll always have enough rewards to pay one year ahead. This value is adjusted daily, so it means there will always exist enough CUBO to pay node holders.

This system also makes the token inflationary if there's new nodes being created, but deflationary if there's an excess of CUBO in the pool. The total supply isn't a fixed number as in other tokens.
